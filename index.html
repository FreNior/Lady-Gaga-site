About her early years as an artist

Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta, also known as Lady GaGa, since her early years, has been a very proeminent singer. At the age of 7, she already was "tapping the notes" on her piano. At 17 she was admissioned in one of the most well kown art school in New York: The "NYC School Of Arts as an early (and very rare) acceptance, but she drpopped out only a year later, claiming that she was better at teaching herself than the school. It was in the balcony there that she witnessed the 9/11 incident. At the 22, she debuted one of the most acclaimed albums of all time, a collection of EDM tracks called Just Dance, and it was her passage to the world of incredible fame.
